About OVERTECH

OVERTECH was established in 2022. Its main type of activity is mineral fertilizer sales. The specialists of the company have the required experience and knowledge in this area.

OVERTECH has direct contacts with global manufacturers of mineral fertilizers.

Warehouse

OVERTECH uses warehouse facilities in ports around the world providing direct access to the end consumer to the goods. It allows to take specific customer needs into account, objectively evaluate and orient better to the local market to implement a more balanced trading strategy.

Logistics, chartering of vessels and containers

To meet the logistic needs of customers, OVERTECH freights and uses different types of vessels worldwide.In addition to our own freight branches, we use the services of international freight agencies and ship brokers guaranteeing competitive rates for the company and customers.
